# Start Here

If you are new, do not try to read everything at once.

Follow this order:

1. Read `MACHINE_SETUP.md`
2. Read `HOW_TO_USE_THIS_REPO.md`
3. Start `html/chapter-01-introduction/notes.md`
4. Move chapter by chapter
5. Build every mini project, even if it feels small
6. After HTML, move to CSS
7. After CSS, move to JavaScript
8. Complete the projects folder in order

## First Week Plan

- Day 1: setup your machine and editor
- Day 2: HTML chapter 1 and 2
- Day 3: HTML chapter 3 and 4
- Day 4: HTML chapter 5
- Day 5: HTML chapter 6
- Day 6: HTML chapter 7 and 8
- Day 7: review and mini project

## Important Mindset

- Do not compare your chapter 1 to someone else’s chapter 100
- Repetition is part of learning, not a sign of weakness
- Small projects build real confidence
- Your job is not to memorize everything, but to understand and practice
